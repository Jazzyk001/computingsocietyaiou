Our Socities
Vision.

To build a dynamic student-led tech community at AIOU where innovation, collaboration, and creativity empower students to grow beyond the classroom and shape the future through technology and leadership.

Our Mission

The Computing Society AIOU is committed to empowering students and supporting the Department of Computer Science by organizing technical, educational, and recreational initiatives including workshops, hackathons, competitions, community projects, networking sessions, sports activities, gaming events, and collaborative programs that promote learning, innovation, leadership, and student engagement.

Membership

Are you eligible
to join?

We do not discriminate based on background, gender, or experience. We only look for passion, interest, and willingness to grow.

  • REQ / 01 Interest in Technology A genuine curiosity for computing, innovation, or digital skills. ✓
  • REQ / 02 Willingness to Learn No advanced skills required — just the mindset to improve. ✓
  • REQ / 03 Team Spirit Ability to collaborate, communicate, and grow with others. ✓
  • REQ / 04 Active Participation Ready to take part in events, projects, and society activities. ✓
  • REQ / 05 Builder Mindset Even small ideas matter — we value creators, thinkers, and doers. ✓
